speaker box project

general information

    The goal of the project is to create a box with the appropriate and optimized frequency response for the amplifier. An optimal volume is given for a sealed box in the data sheet, however, due to the shape constraints of the car's interior, many calculations are required to determine the volume of the desired box. Wood volume is considered and subtracted from the calculated external volume. Pictures of the construction phases will be posted as they become available.
