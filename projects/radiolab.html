radio lab project

general information

Description: The objectives are to understand and construct the various components or modules needed to realize an AM radio, employing a variety of devices including diodes, transistors, and antennas. In some cases, several versions of a particular module (the audio amplifier, for instance) will be studied via PSpice and breadboarding. Based on this study, the best option will be selected. Students will also be tasked with enhancing their radio, and will present the details of this task to their lab section.
