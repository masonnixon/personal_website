fundamentals of electromagnetics for wireless applications project

general information

Description: Maxwell's equations are used in the study of plane waves, guided waves, fiberoptics, electromagnetic compatibility and interference, antennas and radiation, and satellite communication systems. The project task is to develop a solution to the given parameters for an idealized stub matching network.
