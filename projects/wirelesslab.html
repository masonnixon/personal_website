wireless lab project

general information

Description: The project will entail a hardware demonstration of some aspect of wireless communications. The detailed expectations will be discussed with each team in the proposal phase. The results will be presented orally and in writing. A reasonable way of capturing the hardware results might be to use a video camera to record a demonstration.

Oral presentation should be 12 minutes with an allowance of +/- 2 minutes per team member. Each team member should speak for an equal amount of time. The written report should include at least 3 archival, peer-reviewed references. (A single book only counts once.) The report should be sufficiently detailed to clearly explain the project results. The report should be formatted in two-column format according to the IEEE authors' instructions found here: http://www.ieee.org/publications_standards/publications/authors/authors_journals.html
