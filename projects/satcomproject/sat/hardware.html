SATELLITE SUBSYSTEMS

Overview

The various subsystems of a space system are responsible for compartmentalized tasks necessary for proper functioning of the system. The subsystems considered for this mission are power, attitude determination and control (ADACs), guidance, navigation and control (GNC), stationkeeping, and thermal environment mitigation.
